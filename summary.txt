## Summary of the article "Microservices and Microservices Architecture"

This white paper by The Open Group SOA Work Group defines Microservices and Microservices Architecture (MSA), explores their core principles and key characteristics, and compares MSA with Service-Oriented Architecture (SOA). 

**Key points:**

* **MSA is driven by the industry's need for:**
    * Agility
    * Cost optimization
    * Shifts between CapEx and OpeEx
    * Cloud computing
    * Internet of Things (IoT)
    * Digital enterprise transformation
* **MSA is an enabler for these needs, but not a silver bullet.**
* The white paper provides a recommended approach and best practices for adopting MSA.
* It also discusses where MSA is the right fit and its role in the Enterprise Architecture portfolio.
